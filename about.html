About

For my project I created a car sales website. I added 3 different models of different cars as well as an animation of the car driving with relevant sound effects.

3D Models

For my car sales website I created three different models of cars in Blender: a sports car, a truck and a hatchback. I also created an animation of the sports cars wheel spinning to simulate the car driving.

Design

I used Three.js to light my scene with a hemisphere light and added a GUI so the user can control a spot light. I added the models into my web page using three.js as well as using Three.js OrbitControls to allow the user to move and zoom in to the model. I added bittons to play the driving animation, change which car was in view, rotate the current model and toggle the current models wireframe.

Integration

I found audio of an Aventador online and used Adobe Audition so that it played in sync with the driving animation when played on my web page.

Interactions

I added a toggle wireframe button to allow users to see the 3D models wireframe. There is also a button to rotate the car as well as a button to change the car currently being viewed. All of the models can also be moved around in their 3D space as well as zoomed in and out.
